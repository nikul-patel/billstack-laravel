## 1. Introduction
Billstack-Laravel is an invoice & billing automation system for small businesses such as caterers, stores, service firms, and freelancers.

## 2. Target Users
- Small business owners
- Shopkeepers
- Service providers
- Account managers

## 3. Problem Statement
Many small businesses rely on manual billing, spreadsheets, or WhatsApp notes, causing errors and delays. Billstack provides digital billing, customer management, and automated monthly invoicing.

## 4. Core Modules
- Authentication & Business Onboarding
- Customers Management
- Items / Services
- Invoice Creation & Templates
- Recurring Profiles (Auto Generate Invoices Monthly)
- Payments & Reports
- Multi-Business Support

## 5. High-Level Architecture
- Laravel 11 backend
- Blade front-end
- MySQL database
- PDF engine (DOMPDF)
- Multi-tenant via business_id

## 6. Hosting
Supports hosting on:
- Fly.io (Free, supports Composer)
- VPS (DigitalOcean, Linode)
- Shared hosting (with local Composer)
